Research

Currently I'm investigating two aspects of low energy quantum gravity. First, I want to understand the quantization of the gravitational field at the null boundary of flat space-time. To do this properly involves an intimate understanding of asymptotic symmetries of space-timme, and may have connections to the idea of flat space holography. Secondly, I want to understand what quantum gravitational measurements in the laboratory setting can tell us. In particular, would measuring gravity-induced entanglement tell us that space-time itself can be put into a quantum superposition?
